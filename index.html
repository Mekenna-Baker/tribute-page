Bob's Burgers Facts
  • January 9th, 2011 - Bob's Burgers first appears on Fox's "Animation Domination".
  • The first episode of Bob's Burgers received 9.39 million viewers, making it the highest-rated series premiere of the season.
  • September, 2014 - A comic book series based on the show released.
  • May 12th, 2017 - The first Bob's Burgers sound track was released.
  • May 27th, 2022 - The Bob's Burgers movie aired.
  • January 7th, 2024 - The most recent episode of Bob's Burgers was aired, as the 11th episode of season 14. There will be 6 remaining episodes in the season.

Description

Bob's Burgers is an American animated sitcom created by Loren Bouchard for the Fox Broadcasting Company. It is centered on the Belcher family—parents Bob and Linda and their three children, Tina, Gene, and Louise—who run a burger restaurant and often go on adventures of many kinds.
